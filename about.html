About Us

ParNav Foundation is an NGO (Reg No. 2018/0217) founded on 4 Apr, 2017 with feeding of hungry stomachs nearby Anand Vihar Terminal.

Since then, we are carrying out different drives of the needy people because watching those happy faces make us feel better.







What we do

ParNav foundation is an NGO aimed to improve needy lives to a minimum level and then, motivate them to earn an honest living by their own.

The activities we carry on are:
Free education to needy students.
Cloth and food distribution from time to time.
We spread awareness about the sacrifice of our army men among school, college and underpriviliged children .Our volunteers gather all the knowledge about any martyr and then deliver a speech in the gathering of the students.
